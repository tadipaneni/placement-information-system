About customer service

Our award-winning customer service team is a key part Byju’s mission to be Earth’s most customer-centric company. The customer service team has a very clear purpose – to prevent and solve problems and delight our customers. Our team supports customers in 16 languages from more than 130 locations around the globe.

Everyone who works for customer service is focused on preventing and solving problems for customers, from designing and developing self-service technologies to building and managing products and programmes to interacting directly with customers.

The customer service technology and product teams build world-class customer relationship management systems and innovative self-service solutions that are used by millions of customers every day on Byju’s websites, shopping apps and family of devices, Byju Echo.

The customer service team is constantly innovating to raise the customer experience bar. We play a critical role in listening to customers and escalating pain points to enable Byju to improve and deliver its world-class customer service.
