Description

Do you want to build next-generation products for Amazon’s brick & mortar stores including Amazon Go, Amazon Books, Amazon 4Star and Whole Foods? We are seeking an experienced Senior Product Manager to join PRIMS team (Physical Retail ' Integrated Management Solution) to lead strategic initiatives.

As a Product Manager - Technical, you will define long-term vision for the technology that enables Amazon’s brick & mortar stores and formulate a multi-year strategy to support business launch and scale plans. You will be embedded with the engineering team to manage their roadmap and make ongoing trade-off decisions by applying business judgement that balances the current year plans, long-term plans and tech architectural investments necessary to keep our stores operational.

To be a successful candidate, you will have a proven track record of building and managing products in a ambiguous, fast-paced environment in which managing multiple projects and priorities is the norm You are self-driven, entrepreneurial, and have strong business intuition and judgement to compliment the diverging business needs and limited data. You will wear many hats and move fluidly between engineering, business, and store operations, effectively communicating with each. You can think big to drive product vision but also have strong bias for action to drive tactical wins and enhancements. You are an articulate, persuasive communicator and a good writer. You work in a highly collaborative environment that’s more a startup than a big company, where people trust and respect you.
